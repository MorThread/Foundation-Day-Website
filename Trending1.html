SOO hails pioneer Doctor of Optometry interns

The School of Optometry (SOO) welcomes its first batch of Doctor of Optometry interns during the SOO's first white gown and white coat ceremonies on August 25, 2023, at Lecture Hall 1 Building 7 in Emilio Aguinaldo College Cavite (EACC) with the theme: "Upholding Quality Optometry Education: Transcending Potentials to Excellence, Service, Virtue."

The event was a combination of three ceremonies: a pinning ceremony for the 4th year optometry proper students, a clinical gown ceremony for the 5th year clinicians who will now start admitting patients into the clinic, and a white coat ceremony for the 6th year interns who will be sent in the field to various eye care practicing sites.

Furthermore, the ceremonies were visited by Dr. Jose Paulo E. Campos, President of Emilio Aguinaldo College, at the latter end of the event in which he gave a short speech regarding the growing demand for eye care professionals.

Additionally, the event was organized by the School of Optemetry Student Council (SOOSC) in conjuction with the Dean of SOO, Dr. Jesicca Flor-Torre from which the oath-taking of the new sets of SOOCS officers and the first transfet of power from the outgoing Central Student Council Board (CSCB) and pioneer president, Ms Loreilyn P. Perlas, to the newly-appointed president, Mr. Angelo L. Yap was also witnessed.

Moreover, EACC expects its first optometry board takers next year with its first batch of optometry interns expected to graduate in 2024
