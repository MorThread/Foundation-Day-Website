#BAR2023 EACC Law Bar Salubong

School of Education (SOE) successfully pinned 630 new Filipino student educators during its pinning and send-off ceremony on September 2, 2023, at the Emilio Aguinaldo College Cavite Multipurpose Hall.

The program was made for the deployment of the education majors for their pre-service teaching: six Bachelor of Elementary Education majors, four Bachelor of Secondary Education majors in Mathematics, three Bachelor of Secondary Education majors in Social Studies, six Bachelor of Secondary Education majors in Filipino, and 11 Bachelor of Secondary Education majors in English. In addition, they will be deployed at Immaculate Conception Academy’s south campus, east campus, west campus, Plaza Science High School, and Emilio Aguinaldo College Cavite Senior High School.

Dr. Servillano T. Marquez Jr., the Dean of SOE implied during his message that “there’s no turning back” as they are nearing the end of their program.

Our legacy is quality “Education,” Dean Marquez added in hopes that the pre-service teachers will demonstrate Emilian core values of virtue, excellence, and service.

Ms. Marie Christine S. Pasco, LPT., an alumna of SOE, emphasized that “we [teachers] are not just knowledge providers; we are role models,”.

Before she ended her speech, she highlighted to the participants that "your journey as an educator is just about to start."
