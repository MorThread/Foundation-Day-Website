#BAR2023 EACC Law Bar Salubong

"Congratulations, future attorneys!" is all you can hear outside the Manila Adventist College as family and colleagues from different law schools welcome their loved ones who finished the last day of the BAR examination on September 24, 2023.

Present during the traditional Bar Salubong are the Emilio Aguinaldo College Cavite Institute of the Law and Justice (EACC-ILJ) Barops Care Committee as they show support to the second batch of EACC-ILJ Bar takers. According to Mr. Mark Anthony Uchi, Head of the EACC ILJ Barops Care Committee, "Ginagawa natin 'to as a form of support in a sense na from the beginning hanggang sa dulo, nandito kami para sa kanila." We do this as a form of support, in a sense that from the beginning until the end, we are here for them.

Bar Salubong is a tradition at the end of every BAR exam; this is done to show support for the examinee, and it is also said that this Salubong is a way of manifestation, claiming that these examinees will pass the Bar.

Coming from last year, when EACC had a 100 percent passing rate and was the number one law school with less than 10 takers, EACC law school hopes for the best for this year as they have five takers.

Mr. Uchi added, "Here in EACC Law School, we are very optimistic, but we are not putting pressure on them as long as they give their best."

Out of five takers from EACC-ILJ, two of them took the examination at Manila Adventist College.

The result of Bar 2023 is expected to be released by December of the same year.
