EAC records 20.07% increase in college and law school enrollees

With around 30 undergraduate programs offered, Emilio Aguinaldo College Cavite (EACC) continues to grow its population as it marks another high enrollment rate for the first semester of the school year 2023 to 2024.

From 7,388 enrollees in the second semester of the school year 2022 to 2023, the EACC Office of the Registrar recorded 8,871 enrollees from college and law school at the present semester, showing a 20.07 percent increase from the previous.

The registrar also noted that 2,868 of the entire population of college and law schools are new and transferee students.

Overall, EACC's entire population including senior high school students in grades 11 and 12 rose to a whopping 13,272, or a total of 10.25 percent growth from the previous semester's record of 12,038 enrollees.

Moreover, EACC expects its first optometry board takers next year with its first batch of optometry interns expected to graduate in 2024.
